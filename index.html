About IEDC CEC

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Center (IEDC) at CEC has been a hub for fostering student-led innovation and entrepreneurial spirit for the past decade. Through workshops, mentorship programs, and collaborative projects, IEDC has empowered a generation of thinkers, creators, and innovators.

This year, as we celebrate our 10th anniversary, we invite you to join us in recognizing the impact and journey of IEDC CEC. Expect insightful talks from industry leaders, hands-on workshops, and networking opportunities that connect students, alumni, and professionals.
